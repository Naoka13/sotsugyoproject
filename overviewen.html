Contents

This website is made as a graduation project by Okamoto and Shinohara of Kobegakuin University students. The theme of the project is “Japanese religiosity and culture”. Many contemporary Japanese live daily life without consciousness of religion, but in fact some Japanese cultures are influenced by it.
On this website, it’s written the relations between Japanese culture and religion, and there are various information about Japanese religion’s history, events, close culture involved in religion and so on. Please read the main subject if you are interested in it.

Purpose

This website is for learning about Japanese religion and culture. A lot of Japanese answer “No religion” when they are asked, “Do you believe in any religion?”. However, a lot of Japanese who answered it visit a shrine on New Year. Also, there are a lot of people who enjoy other religious events than the above without consciousness of religion in Japan. By learning about Japanese culture that is involved in religion, we aim to get Japanese youth interested in religious views in Japan.
In addition, we’d like to get not only Japanese youth but also foreigners interested in it.
